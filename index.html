Digital Operator Studio is a growing library of AI-powered tools built specifically for the way founders and small teams actually work - fast, scrappy, and without a data team to lean on.

Each kit is a focused tool for one job. Drop in your data, get a structured output, make a call. No subscriptions, no dashboards, no setup overhead.

Kit 01
01

Feedback Synthesiser

What it does

Takes raw customer feedback from any source and returns a structured analysis - themes, sentiment, and prioritised recommendations - in under 30 seconds.

Who it's for

Founders who collect feedback but don't have time to read all of it. Or who read it, but struggle to turn it into a clear next action.

What makes it useful

Upload a CSV with context columns like MRR or plan tier and it surfaces segment-level patterns - the kind of insight that usually takes a spreadsheet and an afternoon.

Kit 02
02

Launch Copy Generator

What it does

Takes your product details and returns submission-ready copy for every directory field - one-liner, short description, full description, tags, and key details - in under 30 seconds.

Who it's for

Founders launching a new product or tool who need to submit to directories but don't want to write the same copy five different ways for every form.

What makes it useful

Generates copy in every length format simultaneously, plus a curated list of the top directories to submit to - so you go straight from copy to submission.

These kits are different. Each one is designed around a specific job that founders actually need done, with a focused interface and output that's immediately usable.

The tools run directly inside Claude - free, with no setup beyond a Claude account. More kits are in development.
