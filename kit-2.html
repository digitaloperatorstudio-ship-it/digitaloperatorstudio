Kit 02

Launch Copy Generator

Everything you need to get your product listed. The Launch Copy Generator takes your product details and returns submission-ready copy for every directory field - in under 30 seconds.

Open the tool →
Before you start

What you need

  • A Claude account - free plan is fine for this tool
  • Your product name and a plain-language description of what it does
  • A rough sense of who it's for and how it's priced
  • Optionally: your website URL, contact email, and what makes it different
Getting started

How to use it

1

Open the Launch Copy Generator

2

Fill in your product name and what it does - plain language is fine, don't worry about making it sound polished

3

Fill in the remaining fields - audience, pricing, URL, and email. The more you provide, the better the output

4

Add a key differentiator if you have one - what makes your product different from existing tools

5

Hit Generate Copy and wait about 10-20 seconds

6

Review the output, then copy each block individually or Download .txt to get everything at once

The form

What each field does

The more you fill in, the more specific the output. Name and description are required - everything else improves the result.

Field What it's for
Product name Used in the output copy and as the filename if you download a .txt
What does it do? The main input - describe what it does and who benefits. Don't worry about polish, Claude handles that
Who is it for? Shapes the audience language in the copy - "solo founders", "freelance designers", "SaaS teams"
Pricing Included in the key details block and informs how the copy frames value
Website URL Used in the key details block - the link directories will point to
Contact email Included in the key details block for directory submissions that ask for it
Key differentiator Optional but high value - what makes your product different. Sharpens the copy significantly
Understanding results

Reading the output

One-liner

Under 150 characters. For directory fields with tight limits - taglines, search results, meta descriptions. The sharpest version of what you do.

Short description

Under 300 characters. One or two sentences. Most directory submission forms accept this length.

Full description

Under 600 characters. Three short paragraphs - the problem, the solution, a closing line. For directories with more space.

Tags

10 searchable tags mixing tool type, audience, and function. Paste them directly into directory tag fields.

Key details

A pre-formatted block with URL, pricing, category, and contact - the fields most directories ask for. Copy the whole block and paste it into a doc to work from.

Getting better results

Tips

Write your description like you're explaining it to a smart friend, not a marketing team. Claude cleans it up.

The differentiator field has an outsized impact on copy quality - fill it in even if you're not sure what to write.

If the output feels generic, your description is probably too vague. Add one or two concrete things the product actually does.

You can run it multiple times with slightly different descriptions and compare - it takes seconds each time.

The Download .txt button gives you a single file with all five blocks, labelled and ready to paste into any directory form.
